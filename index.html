Who can use Paytithe?

Any registered individual can use the paytithe web platform and mobile app for free to make payments. However, only churches can receive payments with paytithe.

How easy?

Simplicity is a big part of the beauty of the Paytithe platform. It really is as simple as\: Launch the app on your device of choice, Choose who and how much you want to pay, Confirm payment. Done.

How secure?

Your data is kept safe and private from unwanted access. Your bank and card details are protected with the maximum encryption algorithm available, to protect you from internet fraud.

Who is behind Paytithe

Paytithe is an initiative of The Sahara Internet. An internet ventures company based in London and Lagos Nigeria. Built with the mind to help people learn about Tithing and Christian Financing.
