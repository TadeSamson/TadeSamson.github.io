About

Give and Receive Donations from Anyone, at Anytime, from Anywhere

Paytithe is an online giving platform, built to encourage giving and to enable the propagation of the gospel and support for social causes.

Churches & Non-Profits

Paytithe makes it easy for your members and supporters to give on the spot or from remote locations. Payments are processed in time, with little margin for error.

Personal Giving

With paytithe, you can give to your church, or adopted non-profit quicky, easily and securely. Just load your debit card details, and you are good to go.

Features

Built to facilitate and encourage giving.

Easy Payment

Making payment with paytithe is as simple as: Launch the app on your device of choice, Choose who and how much you want to pay and confirm payment.

Easy to Setup

You do not need a to have an existing church management system or website before you can use paytithe. All you need is to complete the signup, and you can start receiving gifts in less than 10 minutes.

Secure & Accessible

The Paytithe app is available across multiple device and size. While your data is protected with the maximum encryption algorithm available, to protect you from internet fraud.

Prompt Payment Processing

Doantions are paid immediately and directly into the organization account. No more waiting to confirm payments.

Less Expensive to Manage

Paytithe is free for givers to use and download, and there are no upfront payments required of churches or organizations. No hosting, maintenance or any hidden charge whatsoever.

Built for Giving

Paytithe is packed with features to enable easy disbursement of gifts and offerings. With the auto pay feature, users scheduled giving to occur at specific intervals, and can cancel at any time of their choosing.
